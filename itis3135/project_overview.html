Outline for Client Project

Project Overview:

Description and Purpose:

This application will be based around Weller's Fine Art Sales Firm, a prestigious art gallery and sales company. Its purpose will be to showcase and sell fine art pieces, promote upcoming exhibitions, and provide a platform for artists and collectors to connect. The website will serve as both a digital gallery and a marketplace for fine art.

Users:

The intended users of this website are art collectors, artists seeking representation, art enthusiasts, and potential buyers interested in acquiring fine art pieces. The platform will cater to both established collectors and newcomers to the art world.

Content:

This website will feature high-quality images of artwork, virtual gallery tours, artist profiles, and detailed information about each piece. It will include pricing information, artist biographies, exhibition schedules, and a secure purchasing system. The site will also maintain an archive of past exhibitions and sales.
